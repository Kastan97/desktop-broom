### Smart Mode & AI

Smart Mode is optional and off by default. When enabled, it sends small text snippets from
your files to the AI provider you choose (Claude, GPT, or DeepSeek) using **your own API
key**. Your key stays on your machine, but that file data does leave your device and is
subject to that provider's terms and privacy policy. Do not enable Smart Mode on sensitive
or confidential files unless you accept this.
